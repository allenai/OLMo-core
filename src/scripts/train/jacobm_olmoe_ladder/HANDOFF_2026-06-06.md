## Ladder Philosophy

Use train-loss U-plots over log LR as the primary control loop. Fit local
3-point or 5-point quadratics once a curve is bracketed; use argmin only as a
quick sanity check. When a curve is still improving at the edge, launch both
nearby bisection points and occasional wider sentinel points to find the upturn.

For larger models, do not start with a static copied LR grid. Estimate from the
275M fitted optima, check transfer with and without the noisy Cx2 point, then
launch a four-point sweep centered around the transferred estimate. The 810M Cx1
result should refine the transfer rule before picking later 810M rungs.

Use EP=1 by default for 275M, 810M, and 1.2B. Tianhua's guidance is that EP
should only become necessary around much larger total parameter counts. Smoke
tests showed EP=1 is viable and faster for 810M/1.2B at the settings tested.
